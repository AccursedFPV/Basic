Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect a custom domain to my website?

To connect your custom domain, go to your website dashboard, click on "Manage" and select "Domains". From there, you can add your domain and follow the DNS setup instructions provided.

What's included in my hosting plan?

All hosting plans include SSD storage, unmetered bandwidth, free SSL certificates, daily backups, and 24/7 support. Premium and Business plans include additional features like priority support and enhanced security.

How do I upgrade my hosting plan?

You can upgrade your hosting plan at any time from your account dashboard. Go to "Plans" and select the upgrade option. Your account will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund.

How do I install WordPress on my hosting account?

We offer one-click WordPress installation. From your dashboard, go to "Manage" for your website, select "Applications" and choose WordPress from the list of available applications.

What is your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all hosting plans. If we fail to meet this guarantee, you'll receive credits to your account based on the amount of downtime experienced.
